1. Kolachi (Karachi)

Located at Do Darya, Kolachi offers a beautiful seaside dining experience with a wide variety of Pakistani, BBQ, and seafood dishes. Known for great ambiance and service.

2. Monal (Islamabad)

Situated in the Margalla Hills, Monal offers breathtaking views of Islamabad along with delicious Pakistani cuisine. It's a perfect blend of taste and nature.

3. Cuckoo's Den (Lahore)

Located near Badshahi Mosque, Cuckoo's Den offers rich Lahori food and artistic ambiance. It’s famous for its rooftop views and cultural interior.
